r_ne

Grammar

Force = r_ne( R0, RR, A0, AA, v0, w0, vd0, wd0, q, qd, qdd, Fe, Te )

Global variables

BB, S0, SS, SE, J_type, Qi,
c0, cc, ce,
m0, m, inertia0, inertia,
num_q, Ez, Gravity

Explanation

Function which computes inversed dynamics by Newton-Euler method.


Details

Let FF0(3 by 3) and TT0(3 by 3) be a force and moment applied to the base, respectively, and tau(num_q by 1) be joint torques.
Return value of "Force" variable has the following form.
Force = [FF0]
TT0
tau
